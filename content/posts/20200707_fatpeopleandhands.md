Today I tried to make the most of the downtime during my remote work from home and squeeze in some drawing practice. I spent a bit of time on fat people and hands, to push myself out of my comfort zone and tackle proportions that aren't the usual standard ones. I learned how important the double chin is, and how key it is to first nail the basic geometric shapes inside the subjects I want to draw.
