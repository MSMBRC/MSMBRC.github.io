Second lesson at the course. I kept working on my Jim Carrey, first fixing the eye spacing because they were way too close. I spent a ton of time on the eyes since they're super expressive and really characteristic in this photo. I also adjusted the lower part of the face — from under the nose to the chin — because that was too narrow too.

After that I could finally start on the shading: I put down a first light layer over the whole face, then gradually added more pencil only on the darker areas, and kept building it up darker and darker until I got this result. I think I'll finish it in the next class, but unfortunately that's in at least two weeks. I really need to make better use of the time between lessons. Maybe I'll start my Guybrush on my own in the meantime.
